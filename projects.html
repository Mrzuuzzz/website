Projects

Here are some of the things I've made like websites, games, and more

Mathology website

For a hackathon in 2022 me and one of my friends were tasked with making a project in under 24 hours. We chose to take the beginner track and make an educational website for math. And so, under the code name of "Horrendous Hackers" we started making a website with very little time to spare and no idea how to host it. In the end, it didn't win any awards but still became a great accomplishment for us.

LockdownLife website

Amidst the chaos from the pandemic in 2021, me and a buddy decided to compete in a competition hosted by our high school called: "HTHS-Columbia Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers Website Design Challenge" After a long time of working and researching we came up with "LockdownLife" which won us first place in that competition.
